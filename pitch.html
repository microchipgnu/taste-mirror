A Claude skill for Viator

Taste-Mirror.

Claude already knows you. We turn that into travel taste.

The hook

Every recommender on the internet treats you like a stranger.

If you use Claude, Claude isn't a stranger.

It's seen thousands of your messages. It knows your pace, what frustrates you, what you double-check, what you let slide.

The problem

Pinterest knows your taste.
Viator has the catalog.
Nothing connects them.

Travelers spend hours translating mood-boards into search queries — and lose the vibe in the translation. "Top things to do in Lisbon" returns the same hop-on/hop-off bus to everyone.

The insight

Two signals, fused.

01 — persistent

Who you are

Pace. Energy budget. What drains you. Hidden anxieties. Blind spots. What you'd light up for. Built once from your Claude Code transcripts, refreshed monthly.

02 — live

This trip's vibe

Themes. Aesthetic. Pace. Social load. What to avoid. Read on the spot from a handful of inspiration photos.

The vibe is the mood. The profile is the person. We match on both.

The killer move

The traveler profile.

Built from your own Claude Code transcripts — already sitting on your laptop.

  • Walks every project under ~/.claude/projects/
  • Reads only your messages — prompts, corrections, "no, not like that"
  • Distills each project into a behavioral sketch via your existing Claude auth — no extra API key
  • Merges everything into ~/.taste-mirror/traveler_profile.md

First run: 30–50 min on ~100 projects. Cached after. The skill reads it but never quotes it back — interior monologue, not a horoscope.

End-to-end

What happens when you drop photos.

01

Read

Claude analyzes the images itself — vibe, pace, social, what to avoid. Loads your traveler profile.

02

Match

Server hits Viator's Partner API: tags, search, filtered by vibe + profile-biased avoids.

03

Curate

Claude picks the top 3, drops generic filler, enriches with full Viator detail, writes reasoning that cites your photos.

Affiliate URL passed through as-is. Attribution preserved. We curate. Viator books.

In practice

Six photos: misty vineyards, hands holding olive oil, a vespa on a dirt road, fresh pasta, a wooden farmhouse table.

Slow. Golden-hour. Hands-in-food. Rural.

Profile says: over-plans transitions. Quietly burns out on social load. Won't ask for downtime but needs it.

→ Private cooking class with the host, not the 16-person tour. Pre-booked transfer. Next morning left open.

Why now

Three things had to land at once.

01

Vision + reasoning in one model

The vibe extraction has to feel like the model gets it. That's perception and curation in one shot — only Claude.

02

A persistent user signal

Claude Code transcripts are a goldmine of personality data the user already produced — for free, at high fidelity. No cold start.

03

Skills as a surface

No app to install. No website to remember. The user is already inside the agent.

For Viator

The right shape.

  • Affiliate-pure. We curate. Viator books. Zero inventory, zero payments.
  • Top-of-funnel demand. Inspiration is where the user starts — we meet them there.
  • The long-tail unlock. Vibe + profile match surfaces the high-margin products with the worst keyword discoverability.
  • Differentiated quality. A user who books from a recommendation that cites their photos and their profile churns less than one who booked off SEO.
